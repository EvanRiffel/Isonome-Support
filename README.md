## Privacy Policy
Last Updated: April 2026

Isonome is designed with a "Privacy First" mindset. Your data belongs to you.

### 1. Data Collection & Usage
* Local Data: All game progress, including your level completions and local Time Trial records, is stored locally on your device using `AsyncStorage`. This data never leaves your device.
* Game Center: If you choose to sign in to Apple Game Center, your Time Trial scores and Infinite Mode scores are uploaded to Apple's servers to facilitate global leaderboards. This data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
* Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, or location data through the App.

### 2. Third-Party Content
* Audio: Background music is used under a royalty-free commercial license from Pixabay.
* Analytics: We may use Apple’s standard App Analytics to monitor app crashes and improve performance. This data is anonymous and aggregated.

### 3. Data Deletion
You can reset your data at any time by clearing the App's storage in your device settings. To remove data from Global Leaderboards, please manage your profile within the Apple Game Center settings.
